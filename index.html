Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions? Find quick answers to common queries about our services and products.

What types of UPS systems do you offer?

We offer a variety of UPS systems including standby, line-interactive, and online UPS systems. Each type is designed to meet different power backup needs, ensuring reliable protection against power interruptions and fluctuations.

How do I choose the right CCTV system for my property?

Choosing the right CCTV system depends on several factors such as the size of your property, specific security needs, and budget. Our experts can help you assess your requirements and recommend the best CCTV solution to ensure optimal security coverage.

What are the benefits of using solar products?

Solar products provide numerous benefits including reduced electricity bills, lower carbon footprint, and energy independence. They are a sustainable and cost-effective solution for harnessing renewable energy from the sun.

How often should I service my RO water purifier?

It's recommended to service your RO water purifier every 6 to 12 months. Regular maintenance ensures that the filters and membranes are functioning correctly, providing you with clean and safe drinking water.

Can you handle large-scale electrical projects?

Yes, we have the expertise and resources to handle large-scale electrical projects. Our experienced team can manage everything from initial planning and design to installation and maintenance, ensuring your project is completed safely and efficiently.

What other electrical products do you offer?

In addition to UPS systems, CCTV installations, and solar products, we offer a wide range of other electrical products including air conditioning units, refrigerators, and various electrical components for residential and commercial use. Our comprehensive selection ensures we can meet all your electrical needs.
